## Approved visual direction

Use:

- warm sand / off-white backgrounds
- deep slate text
- near-white cards
- deep teal primary actions
- signal orange only for next actions, attention markers and important highlights
- rounded dashboard / app frames
- practical card-based UI
- subtle four-dash motif representing the four system buckets
- mature product / editorial style

Avoid:

- aviation imagery
- planes
- wings
- cockpit
- control tower
- captain language
- cartoon visuals
- generic SaaS gradients
- harsh neon colours
- large rainbow blocks
- charity / welfare clichés
- productivity-bro language
- invented brand slogans

## Four Life Pilot system buckets

The four-dash motif represents:

1. Money Life
2. Work Life
3. Home Life
4. Support Life
